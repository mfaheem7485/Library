Help

Certainly! Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) along with their answers for the library website's help page:

1.How do I get a library card?

To obtain a library card, simply visit our library's circulation desk with a valid form of identification and proof of address. Our staff will assist you in filling out a registration form, and your new library card will be issued to you on the spot.

2.What are your opening hours?

Our library's opening hours vary depending on the day of the week and any special holidays or events. You can find our current opening hours listed on the "Hours & Location" page of our website.

3.How can I renew or reserve books online?

You can renew books online by logging into your library account on our website and selecting the items you wish to renew. To reserve books, simply search for the desired title in our online catalog and click the "Place Hold" button. You will be notified via email when the book is ready for pickup.

4.Do you offer e-books and audiobooks?

Yes, we offer a wide selection of e-books and audiobooks through our digital library platform. You can access these resources using your library card and a compatible device such as a smartphone, tablet, or e-reader.

5.Can I access online databases and resources remotely?

Absolutely! Our library provides remote access to a variety of online databases and research resources. Simply log in to your library account on our website to access these resources from anywhere with an internet connection.

6.How do I use the library's Wi-Fi?

To connect to our library's Wi-Fi network, simply select the "Library-WiFi" network from the list of available networks on your device. No password is required, but you may need to accept our terms of service to complete the connection.

7.How can I contact the library for further assistance?

You can contact our library staff by filling out the contact form on our website's "Contact Us" page. Alternatively, you can call us during our operating hours or visit us in person at the library. We're always happy to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have!
